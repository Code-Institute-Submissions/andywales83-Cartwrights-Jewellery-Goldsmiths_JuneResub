About Us

We are a well established jewellers and goldsmiths with two award wining studios located in Porthcawl and Porth Rhondda, that specialise in handmade jewellery, designed and manufactured by our goldsmiths on site. We also offer a comprehensive repair & alteration service on all types of jewellery.

As well as our handmade jewellery we also stock a wide variety of other Gold, Platinum & Silver Jewellery, including designer silver ranges.

Our original studio was opened in Porth in the heart of the Welsh valleys in 1989 and our more recent flagship studio is located in the beautiful sea side town of Porthcawl.

We are regarded as one of Wales's best jewellers and goldsmiths winning many awards and accolades over the years and boasting a huge loyal customer base all over the UK.

Bespoke Design Services
